Smartball devices offer a lot of possibilities and are continuously evolving with its community. As of today, they can be driven with the following methods.

Smartball protocol

Smartballs use UDP specific datagrams to communicate over WiFi networks. This bidirectional protocol allow receptions and sending beetween the balls and your client application.

  • UPSTREAM:
    • listening on port 8000
    • subscribers of multicast address 239.0.0.50
  • DOWNSTREAM:
    • sending to port 9000
    • send to multicast address 239.0.0.51

Please see datagram specification for more details.
